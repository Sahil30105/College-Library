V.K.Krishna Menon College of Commerce & Economics was inaugurated on 03-07-1982 by Late Sharad Chandrika Patil, then Education Minister, Government of Maharashtra, when Late Sharad Shankar Dighe, then speaker of Maharashtra Assembly presided. The college was a lone Gift given by the Government of Maharashtra to meet the needs of Bhandup and surrounding areas on the request of Late. Adv. Sharad Shankar Dighe who guided the destiny of the society as Chairman of the College Advisory Board from 1980 to 1986 and as President of the Society from 1987 till his demise in April, 2002.
